SecretBit Ventures (SBV) is an independent consulting firm specializing in privacy-by-design systems and advanced cryptographic technologies. We partner with leading universities, government agencies, emerging tech organizations, and enterprise clients to solve complex privacy challenges through innovative technical solutions.

Our approach combines deep analytical expertise with practical implementation, delivering privacy-preserving AI systems that enable secure data collaboration, compliant machine learning development, and robust protection of sensitive information. From startup advisory to executive strategy, we make complex privacy technologies and AI solutions accessible and actionable for diverse organizational needs.

Key Expertise Areas

  • Privacy Technologies: Homomorphic encryption, multi-party computation, differential privacy, federated learning, and trusted execution environments
  • Strategic Consulting: Technical architecture review, startup advisory, project oversight, and privacy-by-design system implementation
  • Executive Communication: Translating complex technical concepts into strategic insights for leadership teams across academic, policy, and technology sectors
  • Applied Research: Bridging cutting-edge research with real-world applications through rigorous analytical and mathematical problem-solving
